Welcome

Sometimes life begins to feel heavy in ways that are hard to explain. You may feel overwhelmed, disconnected, or unsure of how to move forward.

Counseling offers a space to slow down and begin to make sense of your experience — at your pace, in your own words.

You don't have to carry it alone.

My Approach

I approach counseling through a posture of "withness" — being present with you in a way that prioritizes understanding over fixing.

My work is shaped by the Mannock Integrated Method (MIM), an approach that integrates psychology, neurobiology, and theology, grounded in the belief that we are wired for relationship and that healing happens in relationship.

Together, we will make sense of your story - gently integrating experiences of trauma, suffering, and disconnection into something more whole.

This May Be Helpful If You Are...

  • Feeling anxious, overwhelmed, or stuck
  • Navigating relational challenges
  • Moving through a life transition
  • Carrying past experiences that still feel present
  • Exploring identity, meaning, or faith
